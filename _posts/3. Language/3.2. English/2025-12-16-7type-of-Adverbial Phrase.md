# 🌟 **부사구(Adverbial Phrase)의 7대 종류**

---

# 1) **시간 부사구 (Adverbial of Time)**

언제? 언제부터? 얼마나 오래?

주요 형태
✔ 부사: today, yesterday, soon
✔ 명사구: last year, this morning
✔ 전치사구: at 5 p.m., in 2020, during the meeting
✔ 접속사절: when he arrived, after she left

예)

* The meeting starts **at 3 p.m.**
* She will travel **next week.**

---

# 2) **장소 부사구 (Adverbial of Place)**

어디서? 어디로? 어디에?

주요 형태
✔ 부사: here, there, abroad
✔ 전치사구: at home, in the room, to the office

예)

* He lives **in Seoul.**
* Come **here.**

---

# 3) **방법 부사구 (Adverbial of Manner)**

어떻게?

주요 형태
✔ 부사: quickly, carefully
✔ 전치사구: with enthusiasm, by hand
✔ 부사구문: in a hurry, in detail

예)

* She spoke **confidently.**
* The task was completed **with great care.**

---

# 4) **이유·원인 부사구 (Adverbial of Reason/Cause)**

왜?

주요 형태
✔ 전치사구: because of, due to, owing to
✔ 부사절: because ~, since ~

예)

* The flight was delayed **due to heavy fog.**

---

# 5) **조건 부사구 (Adverbial of Condition)**

언제 ~할 때? 어떤 조건에서?

주요 형태
✔ 부사절: if ~, unless ~

예)

* The system will shut down **unless updated.**

---

# 6) **목적 부사구 (Adverbial of Purpose)**

무엇을 위해?

주요 형태
✔ 전치사구: for safety, for this purpose
✔ to부정사: to improve accuracy
✔ 부사절: so that ~

예)

* He worked overtime **to finish the project.**

---

# 7) **정도·수량 부사구 (Adverbial of Degree/Quantity)**

얼마나? 어느 정도?

주요 형태
✔ 부사: very, quite, too, almost
✔ 전치사구: by 30 percent, to a great extent
✔ 비교 표현: as much as, more than ~

예)

* Sales increased **by 30 percent.**
* He was **as busy as** ever.

---

# 🌟 추가로 알아두면 좋은 것

## ✔ 어떤 꼴이든 **동사를 꾸미면 부사적 기능**

* 명사구도
* 전치사구도
* 부사절도
* 현재분사구도
  부사구로 쓰일 수 있다.

예)

* He walked **five miles.** (명사구지만 부사적 = 어떻게 걸었는가?)
* She arrived **after the meeting.**
* He spoke **while eating.**

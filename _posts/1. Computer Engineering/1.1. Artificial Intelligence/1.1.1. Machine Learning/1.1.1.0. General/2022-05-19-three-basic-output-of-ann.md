### Introduction

Subdividing the Output of Artifical Intelligence Algorithms consists of [Regression Analysis](https://maizer2.github.io/2022/05/15/What-is-Regression.html), Binary Decision, Decision Classification

### Regression Analysis

Regression Analysis estimates one feature value as a number and outputs it.

### Binary Decision

Binary decision chooses between True or False and outputs it.

### Decision Classification

Decision classification chooses a few candidates and outputs it.
